Professor Gleep Glorp (Kiki)
"Suffering from frequent night terrors about eating worms, our protagonist Oliver Goes to tell his best friend since preschool about his sudden loss of appetite, however Blanche seems to have bigger news to share. Blanche has always been a bit of a conspiracy theorist so when she claims that their psychology professor, Dr. Gleb, is actually an alien in disguise Oliver Doesn't take her claim seriously, but after her emphatic insistance, he agrees to "help her catch him" if only to ease her mind. With a very lighthearted goosebumps feel, The two engage in fairly whimsical yet increasingly high stakes tactics gathering more and more evidence that Dr. Gleb is not from earth, however when they are caught staking out his home, he gives rational explanations for all of the phenomenon and they share a laugh. It is only when Dr. Gleb invites them to a good spirited lunch, that Oliver Realizes he hasn't eaten anything in days... horror ensues."
